⚔ Basic Mechanics

Click on a monster to attack it. Each click deals damage equal to your weapon's damage range plus any bonus from the Skill Tree. A 0.5-second cooldown applies between clicks.

Killing monsters rewards Gold and EXP. Gold buys weapons and Skill Tree upgrades. EXP fills your XP bar and levels you up.

The HP bar color under each monster indicates its health:

🌴 Offline Progress

If you have at least one automation active (Auto Attack, Auto Fireball, or Auto UE), the game simulates your progress while you're away — up to 8 hours.

When you return, a pop-up shows how much Gold, EXP, and kills you earned offline. The gains are added to your character automatically.

Offline progress requires your character to be saved to the cloud (registered account).

📣 Major Update Tonight — 9:00 PM CET

The biggest patch since launch goes live today, 16 March 2026 at 9:00 PM CET. A brief server restart may occur — your progress is always saved.

🗺️ 11 Locations & Expanded Late-Game

  • The world now has 11 unique locations, up from 2. Each zone has its own monster roster, difficulty level and loot table.
  • A proper endgame has been added — high-level players finally have new territory to conquer.

🌳 Completely Revamped Skill Trees

  • General, Knight and Sorcerer trees have been redesigned from the ground up — new abilities, sensible costs and a clearer power progression from level 1 to max.
  • General tree gains two new capstone abilities: Fireball Annihilation (C3 — chance to instantly kill all non-boss targets) and Ember of Renewal (C4 — chance to instantly reset Fireball cooldown).
  • Sorcerer tree: Heavy Magic Missile (S2) now meaningfully upgrades missile damage; Sudden Death (S11) cooldown scales down to 2.5 minutes at max rank; Essence Gathering (S12) grants double damage from all sources for 30–60 s after every boss kill.
  • All skill costs across General, Knight and Sorcerer trees have been rebalanced for a smoother, fairer progression curve.

⚖️ Class Rebalance

  • Knight is the active-play class — regular clicking unlocks its full potential through Combo Meter, Adrenaline Reset, and Cleaving Blows.
  • Sorcerer is the passive / AFK class — Heavy Magic Missile, Essence Gathering and Sudden Death deliver competitive output with no manual input required.
  • Both classes reach similar overall gold and experience output at max investment, eliminating the previous gap.

🎒 Loot & Crafting Overhaul

  • A simpler, more scalable crafting system — material drops are more consistent and recipes make sense across the entire game.
  • Crafting rewards now scale properly into the late-game zones.

🔒 Improved Security

  • Multiple server-side anti-cheat measures have been introduced. Exploiting the game should be significantly harder going forward.

Full technical patch notes will follow after the update is live.

About Rotworm Killer

You are deep underground. The torches flicker. Somewhere in the dark, a rotworm is crawling your way. You raise your sword — and click.

That feeling. That's what this is about.

The Inspiration

I've been playing Tibia since version 7.1 — it is, without exaggeration, the game of my life. There is something magical about that world: the atmosphere, the danger, the satisfaction of grinding your way up from nothing. No hand-holding, no tutorials. Just you, a sword, and creatures that want you dead.

Rotworm Killer is my humble attempt to bottle that feeling and make it accessible to everyone — even those who can't commit hours at a time to an MMO. This game is for everyone who loves watching numbers go UP.

Who Made This

Hi, I'm Jakub — 35 years old, from Poland, and definitely not a professional game developer (or any kind of IT professional, for that matter). I built this entirely in my spare time, with vanilla HTML, JavaScript, and Python — no frameworks, no engine, just code and stubbornness.

If something breaks, I'm probably already aware of it and frantically fixing it. Come back in a few hours : )
